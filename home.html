Foodies House, one of the most celebrated restaurant in Patna, has welcomed guests to enjoy its globally inspired cuisine and hospitality since opening in October 2020. Located in the iconic Kankadbagh area of Patna, Foodies House has been a trailblazer within the national restaurant community. From introducing the concept of small and large plates to providing a true farm to table experience with produce from its own farm, The Table continues to be at the forefront of Indian hospitality. With an ingredient-driven focus, global accents and nuances, the menu is a commitment to simplicity and purity of flavour.

The menu is an expression of the finest available vegetables, meat and fish, nearly all of which we procure locally and at the peak of the season.
